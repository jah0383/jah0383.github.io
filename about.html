AboutMe

I'm a computer engineer based in Denver, CO. I'm currently workign at Mile High Millwrights & Maintenance, doing industrial and electrical control systems work.

My main technical interest is AI safety, specifically how LLMs deceive, persuade, and reason under adversarial conditions. I'm currently working on a chess-based experiment studying multi-agent deception, similar to Under the Influence: Quantifying Persuasion and Vigilance in Large Language Models.(Robinson et al, 2026)

I've been running the same weekly 'D&D campaign for 7 years, which has been a huge creative outlet and source of joy for me. I also have a long-standing love of theater and acting, which I pursued in college and hope to get back into at some point. I also like to make visual things, check out the "art" page

I graduated from Bucknell University in 2023 with a B.S. in Computer Engineering, Cum Laude, with a Mathematics minor. I hold a CCNA certification and an OSHA 30 certification.
